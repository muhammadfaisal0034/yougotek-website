# You Go Tech - Domain Context

## Glossary

- **Digital Engineering**: The service pillar focused on building custom web applications, scalable platforms, and robust software solutions.
- **AI Automation**: The service pillar focused on integrating intelligent agents, optimizing workflows, and reducing manual business processes using AI.
- **Growth Strategy**: The service pillar focused on digital marketing, social media strategy, and helping small businesses scale online.
- **Narrative Funnel**: The structure of the landing page, designed to guide a user from initial problem awareness (why digitize?) through service discovery, interactive ROI calculation, and finally to booking a strategy call.
- **ROI Calculator**: An interactive lead-generation widget demonstrating potential time/cost savings achieved via automation and digital solutions.

## Core Concepts

- **Target Audience**: Initially businesses in KSA and Canada, with global scaling aspirations. Target demographic includes small businesses looking to grow and enterprises needing automation.
- **Brand Vibe**: Professional, highly interactive, mindblowing aesthetics (using 3D and complex animations), and authoritative.
- **Design Psychology**: 
  - Utilizing an "OLED Black" base for infinite depth, reducing cognitive load.
  - Interactive "Aurora" gradients (Coral, Chartreuse, Ethereal Blue) that shift based on user input, creating a visceral, living interface that prolongs user engagement and subconsciously builds trust.
  - Large, highly-legible modern typography to assert authority and cutting-edge expertise.
