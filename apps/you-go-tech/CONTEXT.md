# You Go Tech - Domain Context

## Glossary

- **Rapid AI Engineering**: The core service pillar focused on building high-performance digital products at 10x speed and superior accuracy using proprietary AI-assisted development workflows.
- **Agentic Workflows**: The service pillar focused on designing and deploying autonomous AI agents that handle complex business logic, reducing operational costs and human error.
- **AI-First Systems**: Custom enterprise software built from the ground up to leverage artificial intelligence, rather than just "adding AI" as a feature.
- **Efficiency Simulator**: An interactive tool (previously ROI Calculator) that allows prospects to visualize the cost and time savings of the You Go Tech AI-assisted approach vs. traditional software development.
- **Narrative Funnel**: The structure of the landing page, designed to guide a user from traditional development friction (high cost, slow speed) through the discovery of AI-assisted leverage, and finally to a strategy call.

## Core Concepts

- **Target Audience**: Initially businesses in KSA and Canada, with global scaling aspirations. Target demographic includes small businesses looking to grow and enterprises needing automation.
- **Brand Vibe**: Professional, highly interactive, mindblowing aesthetics (using 3D and complex animations), and authoritative.
- **Design Psychology**: 
  - Utilizing an "OLED Black" base for infinite depth, reducing cognitive load.
  - Interactive "Aurora" gradients (Coral, Chartreuse, Ethereal Blue) that shift based on user input, creating a visceral, living interface that prolongs user engagement and subconsciously builds trust.
  - Large, highly-legible modern typography to assert authority and cutting-edge expertise.
